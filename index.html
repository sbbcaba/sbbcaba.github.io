Closing the Loop

Perform these steps only once the official approved authorization is received from the payor.

1. Header Update

Open the placeholder auth. Replace "PLACEHOLDER" with the real number and Uncheck the Placeholder box.

2. Item Update

You must navigate into each Item and update the Auth Number there too. It does not update automatically.

3. Unit Sync

Adjust the Total Units from your 30-day "buffer" amount to the full amount granted by the payor.
